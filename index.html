The Problem

As Arctic strategic importance accelerates — contested shipping corridors, energy reserves, and northern flank defense — autonomous systems are being deployed into environments that commercial AI compute was never designed for. Thermal cycling between Arctic day and night causes solder joint fatigue and seal failure. Standard enclosures throttle or fail entirely. No COTS solution addresses this gap.

Narvalen builds thermally hardened enclosures with active thermal management around leading-edge COTS AI compute boards. Magnesium alloy construction, MIL-spec connectors, conformal coating, and precision thermal firmware keep inference running at temperature extremes where every other solution fails.
